# ⛳ Yardage Book

A personal golf distance tracker built for TopTracer range data.

Track every shot with full stats: carry distance, ball speed, launch angle, height, landing angle, hang time, curve and offline data. Automatically detects mishits, tracks streaks, and shows your progress over time.

## Features
- 📋 Log shots individually or bulk-paste from TopTracer screenshots
- 📊 Full stat breakdowns with optimal launch angle analysis
- 📈 Trends over time — carry, mishit rate, ball speed
- 🎯 Shot shape map — top-down visual of your shot dispersion
- 📐 Club gapping analysis
- ⚖️ Session comparison
- 🔥 Streak & personal best tracking
- 💾 Export/import JSON — your data, your device
